Instructions

1) Open Calculator.html.
2) Choose your course from drop down list. You can choose a maximum of 5 courses but there is no lower limit.
3) After choosing course, choose all sections of that course that you want to consider in your timetable.
4) After choosing courses and subjects, scroll down to the table with checkboxes in it. Each cell corresponds to a specific period of day. If you don't want a class at that time simply uncheck the checkbox at that position.
5) Finally click GENERATE and all feasible clashless timetables will be generated.
